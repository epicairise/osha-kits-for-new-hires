Frequently Asked Questions

Do new hires need OSHA training on their first day?

While not all roles require full OSHA 10 certification immediately, OSHA mandates that employees receive appropriate safety training before exposure to workplace hazards.

What should an OSHA compliance kit include for new employees?

A solid OSHA kit often includes PPE, safety orientation materials, compliance posters, hazard communication training, and certification tracking tools.

Can I use online OSHA training to onboard warehouse or field employees?

Yes — many workplaces use accredited online OSHA courses, often paired with in-person walkthroughs, to meet compliance and save time.
